# Glossary

## Season
A single year of the Dump Cup tournament (e.g., 2026).

## Team
A drafted team for a given season (e.g., Team Dresden).

## Player
A tournament participant.

## Roster
A player's assignment to a team for a season.
Includes effective_from and effective_to for trade tracking.

## Effective Dating
Method of tracking roster changes over time.
Used so that points earned before a trade remain with the old team.

## Match Type
Defines:
- Number of players per team
- Handicap allowance %
- Rank weighting rules

## Handicap Allowance
Percentage applied to a player’s handicap (e.g., 90%).

## Allocation Weight
Percentage of team handicap attributed to each ranked player.

## Match
A head-to-head competition between teams.

## Gross Score
Raw strokes.

## Net Score
Gross score minus team handicap.

## Tournament
Special in-season event:
- Kick Off (medal)
- Midseason (Ryder style)
- Year End (head-to-head)

## Tournament Entry
Player-level entry in a medal-style tournament.

## Tournament Match
Match that belongs to a tournament.

## Award
Recognition given per season (MVP, League Winner, etc.)

## Celebrity Player
Default placeholder player for non-tournament participants.
